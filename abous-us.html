Exceptional Care at Home

Are you looking for a provider of medical and non-medical services at home? You’ve come to the right place. At COUNTRYWIDE HOME CARE, INC., we take pride in helping each individual under our care to improve their daily living experiences. Our caregivers are dedicated and passionate in serving those who are in need.

Phylosophy

Our goals and services are based on two fundamental philosophical principles: the belief in the innate worth of the aged and disabled individual and the belief that everyone, regardless of age, race, color, creed, sex, national origin or handicap (s) is entitled to maximize his potential as a human being and as a member of society. It is the contention of this agency that the aging process is a normal state in the development of any individual and that chronic disease and disability are, to some degree, a part of that process. This Agency is dedicated to care for aged and disabled individuals within the confines of their residence, in order that they may maximize their contributions and fulfill their goals as a family member and member of society with a minimum of conflict. In accomplishing this end, it is felt that the self-respect of the individual can and will be enhanced.

Our Agency is also a vital member of its community. As an employer, the Agency practices non-discrimination and strives to provide opportunities for personal and professional growth. As an integral part of the business community, the Agency makes every effort to serve the people with attention to current area practices and specific needs. Our Home Care Agency implements programs and services to provide comprehensive quality home care in the patient/client’s home. All services are provided under the direction of the Administrator and the Director of Nursing. We address the patient/client’s unique psychological, safety, self-esteem, and self-actualization needs in a client plan of home care.

The Agency

  • serves as a resource for the client, the client's family, and the community. We provide care education for the client, the client's family and the community. These services are provided by Registered Nurse.
  • implements methods of screening applicants and evaluating their progress regularly in order to be able to provide quality personnel.
  • provides staff developmental opportunities and programs, including orientation and programs concerning current practices in care.
  • provides current and accurate personnel records, including verification of current license/certification and health records.
  • maintains open communication with clients, employees, and physicians via frequent contact in order to achieve quality and cost-effective care.

Mission Statement

The Agency's mission is to provide services to clients in their homes assisting them to achieve the highest level of potential in their day-to-day self-care activities. We are committed to providing high quality, multidisciplinary care by professionals who recognize the need for comprehensive assessment of needs from both the client and professional's point of view

Our Agency continues to adhere to standards set forth by Federal, State, Local and Accrediting Bodies. The Agency has approved written vision and mission statements that contain the Agency's operating purpose, philosophy and operating ethics. The vision and mission statements are distributed to all employees, volunteers and members of the Agency's Board of Directors. The mission may be amended only through proper approval by the Agency's governing authority. The vision and mission statements apply to and govern all the activities of all its programs It shall at all times be the policy of the Agency to strive to render home care services according to the highest ethical standards.
